What is the definition of Streaming?

- Live Streaming is a real-time broadcast that allows users to watch or listen to media content, unlike pre-recorded content. It's popular for communication with family, friends, and celebrities. Public live streaming applications include Facebook Live, Twitter Spaces, Instagram Live Stories, V Live, Tik Tok, and YouTube Live, while private live streaming applications include Skype and Zoom.
